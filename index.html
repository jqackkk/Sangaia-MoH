Mission Statement

The Ministry of Healthcare is dedicated to the preservation of life and the promotion of vigor among the citizenry. We oversee disease control, state-sponsored healthcare eligibility, and the administration of Virtue Clinics across all provinces.

Health is not merely the absence of infirmity, but the presence of strength to serve the Aeretocracy.
