Universal Coverage

The People's Healthcare Program (PHP) ensures that no citizen is left without necessary medical attention. Funded by the common treasury, this program covers preventative care, emergency surgery, and long-term vitality treatments.

Eligibility

  • Must be a recognized Citizen of the United Provinces.
  • Must maintain a clean Virtue Record.
  • Must participate in annual health screenings.
